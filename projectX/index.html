Personal Statement

The world in front of my eyes might be a regular day-to-day exhibit of my life although, for many people, it could be a world of their imagination. It resides amid not only a fertile land of farming with breathtaking scenery but also the cosmopolitan city of hectic city life. I have always wondered, how would the lives of those living in a completely different setting say, a terrain? At the moment, the world is constantly evolving, trying to improve every day. On the modern side, there are highways, skyscrapers, office buildings and all the requirements of modern life. On the other side, there is a farm with diverse flora and fauna. But lastly, the sun rises every morning, for a new beginning.
